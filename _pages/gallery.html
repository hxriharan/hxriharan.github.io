About This Gallery

This gallery showcases a collection of images that I find personally meaningful and visually striking. Each piece represents something unique - whether it's the technical precision of computer vision markers, the artistic flow of digital compositions, or the abstract beauty of color and symmetry. These images reflect my interests in both the technical and aesthetic aspects of visual computing, from ArUco dictionaries used in computer vision to the soothing patterns that catch my eye. It's a mix of the analytical and the artistic - the kind of visual elements that inspire my work in computer vision, robotics, and machine learning.
